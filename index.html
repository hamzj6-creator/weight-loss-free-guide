The Ritual

Six checkpoints, spread across your day

This is written as a sequence on purpose — each checkpoint sets up the next one. Missing a couple is fine. Hit all six most days and the compounding is where the real, lasting change comes from. Prefer not to plan it yourself? A guided program like Weight Loss 2.0 turns this exact sequence into a personalized daily plan and tracks it for you.

01On waking

Rehydrate before you caffeinate

A full glass of water first thing wakes up digestion and clears out the "hunger" that's often just mild overnight dehydration. Coffee comes after, not instead of.

02Breakfast

Lead every meal with protein and fibre

Eggs, yoghurt, oats, lentils — whatever fits your kitchen. Protein and fibre are what actually keep you full for hours, which is what stops the 11am snack spiral.

03Midday

Move for 20–30 minutes, at any pace

A brisk walk counts, full stop. You don't need a gym membership to start — you need consistent daily movement your body can actually recover from tomorrow.

A NOTE ON HOW TO USE THIS LIST
043–4x / week

Add resistance training

Bodyweight squats, resistance bands, or free weights — muscle is what keeps your metabolism higher even while you rest. This is the step most people skip, and the one most people end up regretting.

05Evening

Eat without a screen in front of you

Distracted eating is the single easiest way to overeat without noticing. Ten device-free minutes per meal is enough to make a real difference.

06Before bed

Protect 7–9 hours of sleep

Poor sleep raises the hormones that drive hunger and cravings the next day. If you fix nothing else on this list, fixing sleep alone moves the needle.
