The Ritual

Six checkpoints, spread across your day

Each one sets up the next. Miss a couple — that's fine. Hit most of them most days, and the compounding is where the real change comes from.

01

Rehydrate before you caffeinate On waking

A full glass of water first thing wakes up digestion and clears out the "hunger" that's often just mild overnight dehydration.

02

Lead every meal with protein and fibre Breakfast

Eggs, yoghurt, oats, lentils — whatever fits your kitchen. This is what actually keeps you full for hours.

03

Move for 20–30 minutes, at any pace Midday

A brisk walk counts. You need consistent daily movement your body can recover from tomorrow, not a gym membership.

04

Add resistance training 3–4x / week

Bodyweight squats, bands, or free weights — muscle is what keeps your metabolism higher even while you rest.

05

Eat without a screen in front of you Evening

Distracted eating is the easiest way to overeat without noticing. Ten device-free minutes per meal makes a real difference.

06

Protect 7–9 hours of sleep Before bed

Poor sleep raises the hormones that drive next-day hunger and cravings. If you fix nothing else, fix sleep.
